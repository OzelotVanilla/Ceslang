Variable
=======================================

Properties
----------

* Strongly typed
* Allows both staticly and dynamicly typed
* Can be `const` (once init, cannot change)

Rule of naming
---------------

* Legal symbols: A-Z, a-z, 0-9, underscore
* Prohibited starting symbols: 0-9

Convention of naming
--------------------

* Use lower case
* Use underscore to split words
